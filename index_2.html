"Welcome to Acorp Executive Suites! I'm [Name]. Have you been here before? Great — let me give you a quick tour and answer any questions. Can I get you some coffee or water while we walk through?"

Opening Procedures — 7:00 AM

  • Disarm security system · unlock all doors
  • Turn on lights and HVAC systems
  • Verify Wi-Fi connectivity across all zones
  • Check overnight incidents and maintenance issues
  • Review daily calendar — tours, events, maintenance
  • Brew coffee · prepare beverage bar
  • Stock pastries, snacks, and beverages
  • Wipe down reception desk and common surfaces
  • Verify restrooms clean and stocked
  • Set ambient music — morning playlist

04 — Member Lifecycle Management
Onboarding · Billing · Retention & upsell · Offboarding
▾

Onboarding Checklist

  • Welcome email sent with access credentials
  • Mobile app downloaded and tested
  • Wi-Fi connection verified
  • 15-min orientation completed with Community Manager
  • Mail forwarding configured
  • Introduced to 2–3 members in similar industries
  • Community Slack invite sent
  • 7-day check-in call scheduled
  • Welcome kit delivered
Orientation Script
"Welcome to Acorp. I'm [Name], your Community Manager. I'm here to make sure you have everything you need to operate at full capacity. Let me walk you through the space and introduce you to a few members."

Upsell Script
"[Member], you've been running meetings here consistently. A private office gives you dedicated space, 24/7 access, and a professional address for clients. Want to walk through options?"

Checkout Procedure

  • Schedule exit appointment with Community Manager
  • Walk through workspace — identify member-owned items
  • Collect keys, badges, and access cards
  • Verify Wi-Fi disconnection and account deactivation
  • Document condition of space (photo if damage)
  • Provide mail forwarding address
  • Send 5-question exit survey
